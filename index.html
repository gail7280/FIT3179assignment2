Summer Olympic Summary (1992 - 2008)

This visualization includes a range of idioms analyzing the medal statistics of summer Olympic games across 1992 to 2008.

This idiom shows the total medal counts of all countries worldwide from 1992 to 2008 Olympic games.

Among all the countries, United State of America has won the most medals with a number of 1311. It is the only country with a total number over 1000.

Germany, Australia, Russia and China has also won impressive number of medals over 500.

This idiom shows the relationship between athlete numbers and medal numbers.

Overall, we can summaries that there is a linear positive relationship between these two features.

By sliding the bar, we can compare the data between each Olympic game.

The permanent members of the United Nations Security Council are chosen as filter in the drop-down bar. By switching the country, we can see the specific data regarding to these countries.

From the 3 donut charts we can see that the top 10 countries of winning different medals are very similar.

Within all the countries, United State of America has won much more medals than other countries.
